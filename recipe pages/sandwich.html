Bacon Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 slices thick white bread
  • 8 rashers bacon (smoked/unsmoked)
  • Butter for spread
  • 4 eggs
  • Tomato sauce (ketchup)

Method

  1. Add the olive oil to a searing hot frying pan, and add all the bacon
  2. Cook until matte pink - cook further until edges are browned for crispiness
  3. When all rashers are cooked, add all eggs and fry until white - keep yolk runny
  4. Toast the bread, butter, and add 2 rashers of bacon to each other slice
  5. Add the fried eggs to each occupied slice
  6. Add sauce and put unoccupied slices of bread on top of occupied slices
